This year, the dHealth conference – a national scientific conference with a focus on digitalization in the medical environment – took place in the historic premises of the Apothekertrakt Schönbrunn \(Vienna\)\. With Andreas Erhardt, Emmanuel Helm, Anna Lin, Andreas Pointner and Andreas Schuler, several \(former\) colleagues of the AIST research group were represented with the following publications and presentations:

- [Andreas Erhard, Klaus Arthofer, Emmanuel Helm\. Extending a Data Management Maturity Model for Process Mining in Healthcare\.](https://ebooks.iospress.nl/volumearticle/62886)
- [Anna M\. Lin, Anja Schwab, Reza Abolhassni, Stephan Winkler\. From Authoring to Evaluating an Electronic Health Quality Measure – Applying Logic to FHIR® with CQL for Calculating Immunization Coverage\. ](https://ebooks.iospress.nl/volumearticle/62852)
- [Andreas Pointner, Oliver Krauss, Andreas Erhard, Andreas Schuler, Emmanuel Helm\. Multi\-Perspective Process Mining Interfaces for HL7 AuditEvent Repositories: XES and OCEL](https://ebooks.iospress.nl/volumearticle/62882)
- Emmanuel Helm: Architecture of image exchange in ELGA \(orgi: Architektur des Bilddatenaustausches in ELGA\)\.

We would like to take this opportunity to thank you for the exciting presentations and discussions on the topic of eHealth\.
