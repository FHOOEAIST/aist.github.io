The team of the AIST research group met outside office hours on September 17 for a team event\. In groups we mastered the parkour in the archery center Breitenstein and shot some big game \(of course only in the form of dummies\)\. In the process, one or the other of the employees suffered some bruises, but all of them were brave enough to endure them\. Afterwards, we ended the day with dinner at the Seminar Hotel Linz AlpenBlick\.
