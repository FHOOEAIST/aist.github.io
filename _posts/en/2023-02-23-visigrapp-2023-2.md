In the last days Christoph had the chance to give two presentations at the VISIGRAPP conference in Lisbon, Portugal\. In the first presentation he talked about the latest results of our research project [AlpinIO](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/en/projects/alpinio), which focuses on monitoring changes in alpine plant species at the International Conference on Computer Vision Theory and Applications \(VISAPP\)\. The second presentation was about his ongoing dissertation within the [BAMBI](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/en/projects/bambi) project, which deals with monitoring animals in forests using camera drones\.

We would like to take this opportunity to thank all participants for their feedback and discussions during the last days\.
