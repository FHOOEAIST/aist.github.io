We would like to say thanks for the invitation to yesterday’s presentation of the Rudolf Trauner Prize 2019\.

Together with our project partners we are proud to have received two of the five prizes for our projects [Drive for Knowledge](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/en/projects/project-formelracing) and [Kimiku](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/en/projects/project-kimiku)\.

Furthermore, we congratulate our former project partner Rewellio \(formerly [psii\.rehab](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/en/projects/project-psii-rehab-2)\) for the received prize\.

You can find more photos at [cityfoto\.at](https://www.cityfoto.at/content/de/fotogalerie/15308?img=1877190)\.
