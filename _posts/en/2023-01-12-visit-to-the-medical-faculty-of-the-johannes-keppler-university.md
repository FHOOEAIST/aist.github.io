Today we had a visit from the Medical Faculty of the Johannes Kepler University at the Hagenberg Campus\. Our colleagues Emmanuel Helm and Christoph Praschl were allowed to present the topics “Process\-Oriented Data Science for Healthcare: Application of Process Mining Technology in Healthcare” and “Identification and Classification of Living Organisms in Forests” within this professional exchange\.
