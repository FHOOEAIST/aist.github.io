For the completion of the students\- and the research project  [Drive for Knowledge](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/de/projekte) of the company [Formelracing](http://www.formelracing.at/), Thomas Peterseil, the owner of Formelracing, invited all team members to visit the [Red Bull Ring](https://www.projekt-spielberg.com/) in Spielberg\.

Start of the trip was at 09:00 in Ansfelden, wherefrom all team members went right after to the Red Bull Ring in Spielberg\. There first of all was well dined in a restaurant overlooking the Ring\. Afterwards a kart racing take place on an indoor kart track at Spielberg\.

After all were exhausted from the race, there was a 2\-hour tour throw the Red Bull Ring, which was really interesting and give some insights behind the scenes\.

Thank you, Thomas, for the wonderful day\. It was an great project compoetion\.
