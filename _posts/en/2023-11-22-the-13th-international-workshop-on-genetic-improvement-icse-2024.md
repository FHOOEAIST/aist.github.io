Join us for the 13th installment of the International Workshop on Genetic Improvement \(GI\) taking place in Lisbon, Portugal, collocated with the prestigious International Conference on Software Engineering, ICSE 2024\. Details: [https://geneticimprovementofsoftware\.com/events/icse2024](https://geneticimprovementofsoftware.com/events/icse2024)

**Event Highlights:**

- **Date:** Workshop on April 16, 2024 \(Tue\)
- **Location:** Lisbon, Portugal
- **Keynote Speaker:** Prof\. Shin Yoo, Head of Computational Intelligence for Software Engineering, KAIST, Korea
- **Special Issue Opportunity:** Authors of selected accepted papers will be invited to submit an extended version to the ASE Journal’s second Special Issue on Genetic Improvement\.

**Important Dates:**

- Submission Deadline: December 7, 2023 \(Thu\)
- Notification: January 11, 2024 \(Thu\)
- Camera\-ready: January 25, 2024 \(Thu\)
- Workshop: April 16, 2024 \(Tue\)

Stay updated with the latest news via our Twitter: [https://twitter\.com/gi\_of\_software](https://twitter.com/gi_of_software)

**Keynote Speaker – Prof\. Shin Yoo:** Renowned expert in software engineering research, Prof\. Shin Yoo’s keynote will cover topics such as efficient mutation testing, code slicing, fault localization, and the application of large language models in advanced software testing\.

**Call For Submissions:** We invite research and position paper submissions on all areas of Genetic Improvement, including but not limited to runtime efficiency, memory and energy consumption, software specialization, and bug repair\. Best paper and presentation awards will be presented during the workshop\.

**Workshop Chairs:**

- Gabin An \(KAIST, South Korea\)
- Aymeric Blot \(Université of Rennes, France\)
- Vesna Nowack \(Imperial College London\)
- Oliver Krauss \(Pattern Mining and Genetic Improvement expert\)
- Justyna Petke \(University College London\)

**Special Issue on GI in the ASE Journal:** Accepted papers have the opportunity to submit extended work to the Special Issue on Genetic Improvement in the Automated Software Engineering journal\. Explore the various applications of Genetic Improvement in both theory and practice\.

**Key Dates and Submission Details for the Special Issue:**

- Submission: July 31, 2024
- Follow ASE submission guidelines
- See [https://ause\-journal\.github\.io/24gi\.html](https://ause-journal.github.io/24gi.html)

Don’t miss this opportunity to be part of the leading workshop in Genetic Improvement, bringing together researchers, practitioners, and experts to explore the latest advancements in software optimization\. Submit your papers and join us in Lisbon for an enriching experience\!
