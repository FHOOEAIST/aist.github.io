At the [HL7 Annual Conference 2015](http://www.hl7.at/das-war-die-jahrestagung-2015-am-18-und-19-maerz-in-wien/) our colleague  [Andreas Schuler](http://ehealth.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/team/) presented our newest project in the field of HL7 FHIR\. [Naked FHIR](http://www.hl7.at/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/16-Schuler-NakedFHIR.pdf) examines the automatic generation of UI elements for resources with the Naked object pattern\.

Furthermore we like to congratulate our student Oliver Krauss for the  1\. place at the HL7 Austria student award, for his master thesis [Automatisierte Erstellung eines Patient Summary durch Verwendung des IHE On\-Demand Document Supplement](http://www.hl7.at/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/09-StudentAward-Schramm-Krauss.pdf)\. This project was sponsored by us and our partner [systema Human Information Systems](http://www.systema.info/)\.
