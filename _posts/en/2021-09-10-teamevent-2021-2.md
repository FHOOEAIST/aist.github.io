Two years after the last team event the team of the AIST research group met again for a common leisure activity at the archery center Stoneface\-Archery\. In three groups we completed the parkour in Unterweißenbach in just under 3 hours and, as at the last event, shot one or the other wild animal\. Not only the targets had to believe in it, but also some arrows were sacrificed\. Finally, we ended the day at the Dorfwirt Raab in Rechberg with a joint dinner\.
