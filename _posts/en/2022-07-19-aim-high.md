Not only professionally, but also in the private environment, away from the scientific work in the research group AIST, it goes high up\. Last Sunday, some colleagues climbed the Großer Schoberstein at the Attersee together\. After the hike, which lasted several hours, they had lunch together and then took a plunge into the cool waters of the Attersee\.
