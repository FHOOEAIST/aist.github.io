Over the past few weeks, we have been delighted to welcome some special international reinforcements to our research group\. Several students and researchers from across Europe are currently actively contributing to our projects as part of internships, bringing valuable perspectives and fresh ideas to the table\.

Ivan Ludoski from the University of Novi Sad in Serbia is supporting us\. Also on site with us are Tamerlan Aslakhanov and Jasper Vandeput from PXL University \(Belgium\), who are intensively involved in our ongoing research as part of their internships\.

Unfortunately, Hugo Markoff from Aalborg University \(Denmark\) was unable to travel here in person\. Nevertheless, he is closely involved in the collaboration remotely and actively contributes to the international exchange\.

We are very pleased about this collaboration and hope that these international connections will continue to grow in the future\!
