This year again, the AIST research group presented projects at the Open Day of the Upper Austria University of Applied Sciences at the Hagenberg campus\.

Our colleague David Baumgartner showed results on video\-based emotion recognition in real\-time\. This year’s visitors made extensive use of the opportunity to have various facial expressions evaluated and tried to trick the recognition with different grimaces\.

The whole thing can be seen on the official Youtube channel of the FH OÖ Hagenberg\.
