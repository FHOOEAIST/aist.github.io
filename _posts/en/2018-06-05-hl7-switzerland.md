Emmanuel Helm was invited to the meeting of the Swiss HL7 TC on 5\.6\.2018 to present the current technical aspects of the Austrian electronic health record ELGA\. A special focus was on the possibilities to enable in the future the inter\-device exchange of DICOM data based on the ELGA infrastructure\.
