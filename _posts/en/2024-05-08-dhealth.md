Together wirh our project partners from Treetop Medical, our colleague Sophie Bauernfeind attended this year‘s dHealth conference in Vienna, Austria\. Within this conference, Sophie had the chance to present parts of our collaborative research project on digitally modelling medical knowledge from unstructured data in order to make it available for patient care by means of digital applications\.
