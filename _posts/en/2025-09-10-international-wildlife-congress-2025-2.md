Our colleague Christoph Praschl recently attended the International Wildlife Conference 2025 in Lillehammer, Norway, where he had the opportunity to present a research paper entitled “Drone\-Based Sampling for Roe Deer Density Estimation\.” As part of this work, he and his colleagues investigated the potential of drones as a complementary tool for wildlife observation and compared them with established camera trap methods\.
