On July 8, 2025, our colleague Gerald Zwettler presented the paper “Human\-Robot Interaction through a Guided Speech Dialogue System: Leveraging Semantic Analysis with Large Language Models” at the AIRoV – AI & Vision 2025 Symposium on AI and Vision\. The paper shows how large language models can be used for semantic analysis to enable a natural, multimodal dialog between humans and robots\. Congratulations to the entire team of authors on this successful publication\!
