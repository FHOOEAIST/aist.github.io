Christoph was allowed to present the scientific paper “Utilization of Geographic Data for the Creation of Occlusion Models in the Context of Mixed Reality Applications” at the XRSalento 2022 conference in Lecce \(Italy\)\. This work was done as part of the [Mixed Reality Coverage Calculation](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/en/projects/project-mixed-reality-coverage-calculation) Project\.
