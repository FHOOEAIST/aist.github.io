One week at the 4th International Conference on Process Mining \(ICPM\) in Bozen/Bolzano comes to an end\. The PODS4H workshop \([https://pods4h\.com/](https://pods4h.com/)\) on process\-oriented data science for healthcare, co\-organized by Emmanuel Helm, was a great success\! Many inspiring talks, great case studies and research papers, and interesting discussions on a new standard for process mining\.
