After more than 15 years, Barbara Traxler is leaving our research group and we are not only losing a valued colleague, but also a wonderful friend\. With her commitment, her expertise and her warm nature, she has enriched our projects and our team in the long term\. Babsi made a decisive contribution to our position in the Austrian e\-health landscape and has been an active member of the board of HL7 Austria for many years, together with several other colleagues\. We would like to thank her for all the unforgettable moments and her tireless support and wish her every success and happiness in the new phase of her life\. Babsi, we will miss you – feel free to drop by again\!
