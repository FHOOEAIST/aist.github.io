Our colleague Christoph Praschl had the pleasure to present two publications on projects of the research group at [VISIGRAPP 2022](https://www.insticc.org/node/technicalprogram/visigrapp/2022/venue)\.

First, he presented the paper “Three\-Step Approach for Localization, Instance Segmentation and Multi\-Facet Classification of Individual Logs in Wooden Piles” at the ICPRAM 2022 conference, which was co\-located to VISIGRAPP\. This work was done as part of the [Woodmaster\+](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/en/projects/project-woodmaster-plus) research project and continues the results of a [previous publication](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/en/2021/09/16/i3m-2021-2)\. On the other hand, Christoph presented results from the [MRCC](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/en/projects/project-mixed-reality-coverage-calculation) research project entitled “Geo\-Referenced Occlusion Models for Mixed Reality Applications Using the Microsoft HoloLens” at the IVAPP 2022 sub\-conference\.

We would like to thank all participants of the respective sessions for the content exchange\.
