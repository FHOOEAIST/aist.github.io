With August, we as AIST research group unfortunately have to say goodbye to three long\-time employees, colleagues and especially friends\. Johann Aichberger, David Baumgartner and Eva\-Maria Spitzer will work at other national and international locations on various topics in the areas of Automatic Program Repair, Machine Learning and Data Science\. We can look back on a wonderful time, both in terms of scientific collaboration as well as in our private lives\. We wish them all the best for the upcoming phase of their lives in different national and international faculties and companies, for their upcoming dissertations and hope to meet again at one or the other conference\.
