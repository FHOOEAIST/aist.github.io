This year’s annual AIST Team Event brought a refreshing change of pace\. Instead of our traditional archery activity, we decided to hit the bowling lanes together for the first time\. The event was a great opportunity to spend time as a team, share some fun moments, and celebrate our achievements together\.

With this event, we also wanted to express our sincere appreciation to all our employees and current interns for their dedication, commitment, and excellent work throughout the year\. And although some of the images might suggest otherwise, nobody was hurt during the event\! 😀

Thank you all for being such an important part of the AIST team\!
