Reinhard Egelkraut from [Compu Group Medical Austria](http://www.cgm.com/us/index.en.jsp) and [Oliver Krauss](http://ehealth.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/team/) attended the January Work Group Meeting \(WGM\) of HL7 for our Project [KIMBO](http://ehealth.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/projekte/)\. A big thank you to [HL7 Austria](http://www.hl7.at/) for sponsoring Olivers attendance\.

At the Connecthaton Reinhard and Oliver participated in the [Attachments](http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=201701_Attachments) track, implementing Placer and Filler, which also helped with understanding the FHIR Workflow Patterns\.

We are looking forward to the next Connectathon and Workgroup Meeting in Madrid – Spain\.
