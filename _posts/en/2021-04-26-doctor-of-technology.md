Our long\-time collaborator, colleague and friend Emmanuel Helm completed his rat Johannes Kepler University \(JKU\) last week with “Very Good”\.

Thus, another stage in his academic career has been mastered and he can fully dedicate himself to his position as a fully professional lecturer at the Department of Medicine and Bioinformatics at the Faculty of Computer Science, Communication and Media in Hagenberg and to his work as a research project leader of the AIST research group\.

We congratulate him on this special success and wish him all the best for the future\!

P\.S\. In old Finnish tradition we were allowed to present a sword for the successful conclusion\.

P\.P\.S\. For the photo, all safety distances regarding Covid\-19 were observed and all persons not present in the photo wore FFP2 masks\.
