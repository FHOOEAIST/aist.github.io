We are proud to announce that **Andreas Pointner**, Research Associate in R&D, represented our organization at the **41st IEEE International Conference on Software Maintenance and Evolution \(ICSME 2025\)**, held from **September 7–12, 2025**, at the University of Auckland, New Zealand\.

At this prestigious event, Andreas presented his latest research titled **“Inferring Attributed Grammars from Parser Implementations”**\. His work introduces an innovative approach to recovering semantic specifications from recursive descent parsers through dynamic analysis, enabling the generation of comprehensive attributed grammars\. This advancement supports improved software maintenance and evolution, reinforcing our commitment to cutting\-edge research and development\.
