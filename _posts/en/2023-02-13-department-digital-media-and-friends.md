Under the motto “Department digital media and friends” a meeting around the department of digital media took place last week at the Riverresort Donauschlinge\. There was an exchange between the research groups [WinLab](https://www.win-lab.at/), [Playful Interactive Environments Lab](https://pie-lab.at/), [Media Interaction Lab](https://mi-lab.org/), [Embedded Systems Lab](https://www.embedded-lab.at/de/ueber-uns/) and AIST with the focus on an even stronger cooperation in the future\.
