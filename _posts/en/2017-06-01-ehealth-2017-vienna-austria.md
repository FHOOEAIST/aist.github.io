From May 23 to May 24 [Andreas Schuler](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/de/team) participated in the eHealth 2017 conference in Schönbrunn castle in Vienna\. Especially the presentations about various topics as part of the scientific track provided some interesting insights, problems and approaches\. Our colleague Andreas Schuler presented the paper [Challenges and Approaches to Make Multidisciplinary Team Meetings Interoperable\-The KIMBo Project](https://books.google.at/books?hl=en&lr=&id=2UXiDgAAQBAJ&oi=fnd&pg=PA63&dq=info:26FxUKEpyYMJ:scholar.google.com&ots=bhfucLCU1O&sig=fPfwQlxuocW2u6SeWbKK7U-23jM&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q&f=false), which summarizes the results of the KIMBo project and discusses a possible solution to an interdisciplinary board meeting solution based on the HL7 FHIR specification and its challenges\.
