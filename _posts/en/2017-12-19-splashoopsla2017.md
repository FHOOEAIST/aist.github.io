From the 22\. till the 27\. of October the [SPLASH/OOSPLA 2017](https://2017.splashcon.org/home) \(Systems, Programming, Languages and Applications: Software for Humanity\) conference was held in Vancouver, Canda\. [Oliver Krauss](http://ehealth.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/team/) did present his PhD thesis there\.

Oliver Krauss presented the paper „[Genetic Improvement in Code Interpreters and Compilers](https://dl.acm.org/citation.cfm?id=3135934)“, which handles the core of his PhD thesis\. The presentations were in the tracks of the Doctoral Symposium, Student Research Competition and Poster Session\. Thank you to all the participants which provided valuable feedback and input for further research opportunities\!
