From May 08 to May 09 [Emmanuel Helm](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/de/team) and [Anna Lackerbauer](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/de/team) participated in the eHealth 2018 conference in Schönbrunn castle in Vienna\. Especially the presentations about various topics as part of the scientific track provided some interesting insights, problems and approaches\. Our colleague [Emmanuel Helm](https://aist.fh-hagenberg.at/index.php/de/team) presented the paper _Cross\-Enterprise Communication and Data Exchange in Radiology in Austria: Technology and Use Cases,_ which describes the current methods in Austria to communicate and exchange data in radiology\.
