This year Oliver Krauss attended ICSE 2019 in Montreal\. He presented his publicaiton “Mining Patterns From Genetic Improvement Experiments” in the GI workshop\. In the workshop a mining of patterns from the interim\-results in evolutionary algorithms \(individuals\) is proposed\. The goal is to mine good patterns concerning non\-functional properties, such as performance and energy, as well as recurring patterns in specific problem domains\.

You can read the publication [here](http://geneticimprovementofsoftware.com/paper_pdfs/krauss2019mining.pdf) and the slides [here](http://geneticimprovementofsoftware.com/slides/krauss2019mining_slides.pdf)\.
