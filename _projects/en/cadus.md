## Cadus

Together with the company Cadus, a system is to be developed that will greatly simplify processes in the construction industry\. Specifically, a software product is to be developed that allows a target/actual comparison of buildings to be created\. This system is to be developed for the Microsoft HoloLens 2, and can be used directly in the building\. A previously loaded construction plan is then to be compared with the respective room scans of the HoloLens in order to recognize different structures\. This will relieve the construction manager of the tedious task of manually measuring the rooms\.
