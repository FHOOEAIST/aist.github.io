## Project Progast+

The aim of this research project is to realize the technological basis for the application development of a digital voucher booklet as a prototype\. Since no hard change from the physical voucher book to the application can/should be carried out, a parallel operation of several years is to be aimed at\. The first development stage, which is addressed in the context of this project, aims at the prototypical implementation of a voucher partner search function \(oriented towards customer profile, behaviour and promotion of companies\)\. The physically printed partner companies are displayed via a user\-specific search and filter function\.
