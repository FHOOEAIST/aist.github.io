## Project: Mixed Reality Coverage Calculation

The research project “Mixed Reality Coverage Calculation” \(MRCC\)” deals with methods for coverage calculation in the environment of outdoor mixed reality applications\. The goal is to create a coverage calculation as accurate as possible based on real objects like houses but also on topographic features \(hills, mountains\)\. This should improve the immersion for users of corresponding devices \(such as the Microsoft HoloLens\) and thus further improve the possibilities of mixed reality applications\.
