## Project Oppa

Due to the demographic development and the increasing number of people in need of care, the demands on care at home will continue to rise, especially for caring relatives, personal caregivers and social and health care providers\. A standardized, IT\-supported recording of the services provided by caregivers and the patient’s state of health promotes the quality of care at home and supports the exchange of information between different actors, beyond the boundaries of care\. The OPPA project is concerned with the conceptual design and prototypical implementation of a platform for caregiving relatives and personal caregivers\. The research group AIST together with the research group PIE \([https://pie\-lab\.at/](https://pie-lab.at/)\) supports the project partners with know\-how in medical standards and UX design\.
