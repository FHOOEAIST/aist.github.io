## Project: Predictive Innovation

To innovate is one of the most important tasks of companies to keep and improve their market positions\. Finding ideas for new products or improving existing ones is a challenging creative task\. mobile agreements \(mogree\) provides the AnyIdea platform for the purpose of helping enterpreneurs find interesting ideas through crowd sourcing\. This research project aims to improve this concept via natural language processing and artificial intelligence\. This is done by identifying current megatrends from research publications, industry whitepapers and news articles, to identify what will be the next big thing in a market area and help innovators get an overview of the target domain\.
