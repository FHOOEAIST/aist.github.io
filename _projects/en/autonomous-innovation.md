## Autonomous Innovation

The so\-called “Double Diamond” innovation process is an established procedural model designed to stimulate innovation within a company\. It involves using a variety of different methods that require a combination of internal and external knowledge, understanding of the task, awareness of the target audience, and much more\. Advances in artificial intelligence in the field of text and image generation now enable support even in complex tasks like this\. Therefore, the goal of the project is to develop a prototype that guides users through the Double Diamond innovation process, supported by AI methods\.
