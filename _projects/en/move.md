## M.O.V.E. - Modeling Orthoses for Vitality Enhancement

In the research project M\.O\.V\.E\. \(Modeling Orthosses for Vitality Enhancement\), the field of orthopedics is to be digitized in the sense of Industry 4\.0\. A self\-learning suggestion system is to be created which, with automated patient\-specific orthosis designs, will lead to significant process automation in the field of orthopedics with the aid of 3D printing\. The digitized orthosis as the objective of this project can precisely measure the pressure exerted during the entire therapy and thus objectify the course of treatment and form an enormous added value as a basis for medical research as well as for the control of the treatment process\.
