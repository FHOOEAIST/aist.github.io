## Project ORG-Manager

The company LMNOP provides a software as a service for the management of clubs and associations\. The so\-called ORG\-Manager offers them the possibility to manage and coordinate their members as well as internal processes and procedures\. In the course of the research project, this is to be extended by further functionalities\. For example, it should be possible to modularize individual components, so that a step\-by\-step changeover for associations is possible\. A central aspect here is authentication \(keyword: OAuth\)\. In addition, a “morpher importer” has to be designed\. This will use artificial intelligence to create the possibility of importing any data structures\.
